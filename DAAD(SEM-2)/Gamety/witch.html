Combat System

Swordsmanship

Fast and strong attacks with silver and steel swords. Master timing and positioning for maximum effectiveness.

Signs Magic

Five magical signs: Igni (fire), Quen (shield), Aard (telekinetic blast), Yrden (trap), and Axii (mind control).

Alchemy

Brew potions, oils, and bombs to enhance combat effectiveness and exploit monster weaknesses.

Defense

Dodge, roll, and parry enemy attacks. Use Quen sign for magical protection and damage absorption.

Crossbow

Ranged combat option effective against flying enemies and underwater creatures. Various bolt types available.

Mutations

Advanced character mutations that enhance combat abilities and unlock new tactical possibilities.

Combat Mechanics

Damage Types

Enemy Weaknesses
