Game Overview

About the Game

Grand Theft Auto V is a 2013 action-adventure game developed by Rockstar North and published by Rockstar Games. It is the seventh main entry in the Grand Theft Auto series and the fifteenth installment overall. Set within the fictional state of San Andreas, based on Southern California, the game follows three protagonists and their attempts to commit heists while under pressure from a corrupt government agency and powerful criminals.

Gameplay Features

Character Switching

For the first time in the series, GTA V features three playable protagonists. Players can switch between Michael, Franklin, and Trevor at almost any time, experiencing their individual storylines that eventually interweave into an epic narrative.

Switch Mechanics

  • • Instant character switching via directional wheel
  • • Dynamic camera transitions
  • • Each character continues their life when not controlled
  • • Unique abilities and skill sets

Combat System

Enhanced combat mechanics featuring improved shooting, cover system, and weapon handling. The game includes a wide variety of weapons from pistols to heavy machinery, each with realistic physics and damage models.

Combat Features

  • • Advanced cover system
  • • Auto-aim assistance options
  • • Weapon customization and upgrades
  • • Stealth mechanics
