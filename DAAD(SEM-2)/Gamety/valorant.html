Game Overview

What is VALORANT?

VALORANT is a free-to-play first-person tactical hero shooter developed and published by Riot Games. Released in 2020, it combines precise gunplay with unique agent abilities in intense 5v5 matches.

The game emphasizes competitive integrity with advanced anti-cheat systems and focuses on strategic team-based gameplay where every round matters.

Players choose from a diverse cast of agents, each with unique abilities that complement different playstyles and team compositions.
