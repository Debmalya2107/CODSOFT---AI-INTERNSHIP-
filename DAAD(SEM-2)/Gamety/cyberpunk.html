THE STORY

Plot Synopsis

In 2077, Night City is a megalopolis obsessed with power, glamour, and body modification. You play as V, a mercenary outlaw going after a one-of-a-kind implant that is the key to immortality. The implant contains the digital ghost of Johnny Silverhand, a legendary rockerboy and terrorist who died decades ago.

As the implant slowly overwrites V's personality with Johnny's, you must navigate the dangerous world of corporate espionage, street gangs, and cyberpunk politics while searching for a way to save your own identity and perhaps achieve digital immortality.
