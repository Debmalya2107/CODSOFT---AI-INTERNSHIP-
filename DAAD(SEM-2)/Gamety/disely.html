Game Overview

Disco Elysium is a groundbreaking role-playing game that redefines what RPGs can be. You're a detective with a unique approach to investigating a murder case in the fictional city of Revachol. What sets this game apart is its revolutionary dialogue system and the absence of traditional combat mechanics.

Instead of fighting enemies, you battle your own thoughts, debate with your inner voices, and navigate complex social interactions. Every conversation is a puzzle, every decision shapes your character, and every thought you think becomes a part of who you are.

NARRATIVE DEPTH

The Investigation

What begins as a straightforward murder investigation quickly spirals into something much deeper. The death of a mercenary in the coastal town of Martinaise becomes a window into decades of political upheaval, personal trauma, and systemic corruption.

"You're standing in the ruins of a miracle. The city of Revachol, a once-great metropolis brought low by revolution, invasion, and the slow decay of time. And you? You're just another broken piece of it."

The game explores themes of addiction, political ideology, personal responsibility, and the nature of change itself. Every conversation reveals new layers of meaning, and every choice you make reshapes both the investigation and your understanding of yourself.
