MINECRAFT

Explore infinite worlds and build everything from the simplest of homes to the grandest of castles. Play in creative mode with unlimited resources or mine deep into the world in survival mode.

Game Overview

Creative Mode

Build with unlimited resources and fly around the world. Perfect for construction projects and artistic creations.

Survival Mode

Gather resources, craft tools, and survive hostile mobs. Manage hunger and health while exploring.

Multiplayer

Join servers or play with friends locally. Collaborate on builds or compete in mini-games.

Mods & Plugins

Customize your experience with thousands of mods, texture packs, and server plugins.

Block Types

Dirt Block

Basic building block found everywhere. Can be converted to farmland for crops.

Grass Block

Dirt with grass on top. Animals spawn on grass and it spreads to nearby dirt.

Stone

Solid block found underground. Source of cobblestone when mined.

Wood Log

Essential resource from trees. Used for crafting tools and building.

Iron Ore

Valuable ore that smelts into iron ingots for advanced tools.

Gold Ore

Rare ore found deep underground. Smelts into gold ingots.

Diamond Ore

Extremely rare and valuable. Creates the strongest tools and armor.

Lava

Dangerous liquid that causes fire damage. Can be collected with buckets.

Crafting Recipes

Wooden Pickaxe

Essential tool for mining stone and ores.

Crafting Table

Upgrade from 2x2 to 3x3 crafting grid.

Furnace

Smelt ores and cook food items.

Biomes

Plains

Flat grasslands perfect for building. Home to horses, cows, and sheep. Contains villages and plenty of space for farms.

  • Abundant grass and flowers
  • Villages with villagers
  • Perfect for agriculture
  • Oak trees scattered around

Forest

Dense woodland areas rich in wood resources. Dark forests contain special mansion structures and dangerous mobs.

  • Oak and birch trees
  • Wolves and various animals
  • Abundant wood supply
  • Mushrooms and flowers

Desert

Sandy wasteland with cacti and rare oases. Contains pyramids with valuable treasures and dangerous traps.

  • Sand and sandstone blocks
  • Desert temples with loot
  • Cacti for protection
  • Villages adapted to heat

Mountains

Towering peaks with stone and ore. Dangerous terrain but rich in valuable resources like emeralds and coal.

  • High altitude terrain
  • Rich ore deposits
  • Snow-capped peaks
  • Challenging navigation

Ocean

Vast water bodies with underwater structures. Home to dolphins, fish, and dangerous guardians protecting ocean monuments.

  • Ocean monuments with guardians
  • Shipwrecks with treasure
  • Coral reefs and sea life
  • Underwater exploration

Nether

Hell-like dimension accessed through obsidian portals. Contains unique materials and extremely dangerous mobs.

  • Nether fortresses with blazes
  • Ghasts and zombie pigmen
  • Glowstone and nether quartz
  • Lava seas and fire

Creatures & Mobs

Creeper

Silent green mob that explodes when near players. Drops gunpowder and music discs.

Health: 20 HP
Behavior: Hostile

Zombie

Undead mob that spawns in darkness. Burns in sunlight and can break down doors.

Health: 20 HP
Behavior: Hostile

Skeleton

Archer mob with bow and arrows. Drops bones and arrows when defeated.

Health: 20 HP
Behavior: Hostile

Pig

Peaceful farm animal that provides pork. Can be ridden with a saddle and carrot on a stick.

Health: 10 HP
Behavior: Passive

Sheep

Woolly farm animal that provides wool when sheared. Wool regrows and can be dyed.

Health: 8 HP
Behavior: Passive

Wolf

Wild dog that can be tamed with bones. Loyal companion that helps in combat.

Health: 8-20 HP
Behavior: Tameable
