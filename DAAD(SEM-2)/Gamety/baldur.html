Story & Companions

The Mind Flayer Threat

Infected with a mind flayer parasite, you must find a cure before undergoing ceremorphosis - the horrific transformation into a mind flayer. Your journey will take you across the Sword Coast, from the city of Baldur's Gate to the depths of the Underdark, as you uncover a conspiracy that threatens the entire realm.
