Gameplay Mechanics

Combat System

Dead Eye targeting system allows for precise shooting. Multiple weapon types including revolvers, rifles, and shotguns each with unique handling.

Horse Bonding

Build relationships with various horse breeds. Each horse has unique stats for speed, stamina, and health. Care for your horse to improve bonding levels.

Camp Life

Manage gang camp with upgrades, contribute resources, and interact with gang members. Your actions affect camp morale and story progression.

Hunting & Fishing

Track over 200 animal species with realistic behavior. Perfect pelts require proper weapons and shot placement. Fish in various water bodies.

Honor System

Your actions affect Arthur's honor level. Help strangers and make moral choices to be honorable, or rob and kill for a dishonorable path.

Survival Elements

Maintain Arthur's health, stamina, and Dead Eye cores through eating, sleeping, and bathing. Weather affects gameplay and character needs.
