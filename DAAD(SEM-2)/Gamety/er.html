Game Overview

Elden Ring is an action RPG developed by FromSoftware, combining the challenging gameplay mechanics of the Dark Souls series with an open-world structure co-created with George R.R. Martin.

Gameplay

Explore the vast Lands Between, an open world filled with mysteries, dungeons, and formidable foes. Master combat mechanics including dodging, blocking, parrying, and magic casting. Customize your character through extensive leveling and equipment systems.

World Design

The Lands Between feature diverse environments from lush forests to desolate wastelands. Discover hidden areas, underground cities, and towering castles. Each location tells a story through environmental storytelling and intricate level design.

Multiplayer

Engage in cooperative and competitive multiplayer experiences. Summon allies to aid in battles, leave messages for other players, or invade their worlds for PvP combat. The multiplayer system seamlessly integrates with the single-player experience.

Major Bosses

Face legendary foes and demigods in epic battles that will test your skill, patience, and determination.

Margit, the Fell Omen

The first major boss blocking your path to Stormveil Castle. Margit is a formidable opponent who serves as a skill check for new players entering the Lands Between.

Strategy Tips:

  • • Use the Margit's Shackle item for easier phase transitions
  • • Attack during his long wind-up animations
  • • Watch for his delayed hammer attacks
  • • Summon Sorcerer Rogier for assistance

Godrick the Grafted

Lord of Stormveil Castle, Godrick is a demigod who grafts limbs from fallen warriors to increase his power. He possesses a Great Rune essential to your journey.

Strategy Tips:

  • • Focus on his ankles in the first phase
  • • Beware of his dragon arm in phase two
  • • Use fire damage for extra effectiveness
  • • Summon Nepheli Loux for help

Rennala, Queen of the Full Moon

The former queen of the Carian Royal Family and master of the Academy of Raya Lucaria. She commands powerful sorceries and cosmic magic.

Strategy Tips:

  • • Target glowing students in phase one
  • • Stay close in phase two to avoid most spells
  • • Dodge roll through her moon beam attack
  • • Magic resistance gear helps significantly

Starscourge Radahn

The legendary demigod general who conquered the stars themselves. Despite being afflicted by scarlet rot, he remains one of the most formidable warriors in the Lands Between.

Strategy Tips:

  • • Participate in the festival battle
  • • Resummon fallen allies throughout the fight
  • • Use Torrent for mobility and escaping gravity attacks
  • • Attack his feet when he's distracted

Lore & Story

Uncover the rich narrative tapestry woven by Hidetaka Miyazaki and George R.R. Martin, exploring themes of power, corruption, and the cycle of civilizations.

The Golden Order

The Golden Order represents the divine rule established by the Greater Will through the Erdtree. This cosmic force brought order and prosperity to the Lands Between, establishing a hierarchy of gods, demigods, and mortals bound by fate and divine law.

Queen Marika the Eternal served as the vessel for this divine power, ruling over the lands with the support of her consort Radagon and their demigod children who each possessed fragments of the Elden Ring.

Key Figures

  • Queen Marika: The eternal ruler and vessel of the Elden Ring
  • Radagon: Marika's consort and alter ego
  • Godfrey: First Elden Lord and warrior king
  • The Two Fingers: Envoys of the Greater Will

The Shattering

The Great Runes

  • Godrick's Rune: Power of grafting and growth
  • Rennala's Rune: Rebirth and transformation
  • Radahn's Rune: Strength and gravity magic
  • Rykard's Rune: Blasphemous power and consumption

The Shattering was a catastrophic war that erupted when Queen Marika shattered the Elden Ring, breaking the divine order and plunging the Lands Between into chaos. The demigod children claimed fragments called Great Runes, leading to endless conflict.

This war corrupted the land and its inhabitants, creating the desolate world that the Tarnished must navigate. Each demigod's pursuit of power led to their own corruption and the suffering of their domains.

The Tarnished

You are among the Tarnished, exiled warriors who lost the grace of gold and were banished from the Lands Between. Now called back by grace after countless ages, the Tarnished represent the last hope for restoring order to the shattered realm.

Your journey is one of choice and consequence, with multiple possible endings that reflect different philosophies about power, order, and the nature of existence itself. Will you restore the Golden Order, embrace chaos, or forge an entirely new path for the world?

Core Mechanics

Master the fundamental systems that govern combat, character progression, and exploration.

Stats & Leveling

Vigor: Health points and resistances
Mind: FP (mana) for spells and skills
Endurance: Stamina and equipment load
Strength: Physical damage scaling
Dexterity: Weapon speed and casting
Intelligence: Sorcery scaling
Faith: Incantation scaling
Arcane: Item discovery and bleed

Combat System

• Light/Heavy Attacks: Basic combat options
• Guard Counter: Counterattack after blocking
• Critical Hits: Massive damage opportunities
• Status Effects: Bleed, poison, frostbite
• Weapon Skills: Unique abilities per weapon
• Dodge Rolling: I-frames for evasion
• Parrying: Timing-based defense

Torrent & Exploration

• Mounted Combat: Fight from horseback
• Double Jump: Enhanced mobility
• Spirit Springs: Vertical traversal
• Stakes of Marika: Boss respawn points
• Sites of Grace: Save points and guidance
• Map Fragments: Reveal area details
• Fast Travel: Instant transportation
