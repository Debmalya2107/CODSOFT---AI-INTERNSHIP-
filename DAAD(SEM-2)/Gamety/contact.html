Frequently Asked Questions

How can I request a new game to be added?

Simply send us an email at content@gamety.com with the game title, platform details, and why you think it should be documented. We review all suggestions and prioritize based on community interest and game popularity.

Do you accept game review submissions?

Yes! We welcome guest reviewers and community contributions. Contact us at submissions@gamety.com with your writing samples and the games you'd like to review. Our editorial team will guide you through our submission process.

Can I collaborate with you for content?

Absolutely! We're always looking for passionate gamers, content creators, and developers to collaborate with. Reach out to partnerships@gamety.com with your ideas, portfolio, and how you'd like to contribute to our gaming community.

Do you provide download links for games?

No, we focus exclusively on providing comprehensive documentation, guides, reviews, and information about games. We do not host or provide download links for games. We recommend purchasing games through official stores and legitimate retailers.

How often do you update your game documentation?

We continuously update our documentation based on game patches, expansions, and community feedback. Major updates are typically reflected within 48-72 hours, while minor corrections are made as soon as we're notified of any issues.
