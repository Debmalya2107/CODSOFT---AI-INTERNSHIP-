Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a reservation?

While walk-ins are welcome, we highly recommend making a reservation, especially during peak hours (weekends and evenings) or for parties of 4 or more. Reservations ensure you get your preferred seating and minimize wait times. You can make reservations through our website, phone, or by visiting us in person.

Do you accommodate dietary restrictions?

Absolutely! We offer a variety of options for different dietary needs, including vegan, vegetarian, gluten-free, and dairy-free alternatives. Our kitchen staff is trained to handle food allergies safely. Please inform us of any dietary restrictions when making your reservation or ordering, and we'll be happy to accommodate your needs.

Is parking available?

Yes, we offer complimentary valet parking for all our guests. Our valet service is available during all business hours. Additionally, there's street parking available on Heritage Boulevard and a public parking garage two blocks away at Heritage Square.

Do you host private events?

We'd love to host your special occasion! The Vintage Café offers private dining for groups of 15-50 guests, with customized menus and dedicated service. We cater to birthday parties, business meetings, wedding receptions, anniversary celebrations, and more. Contact our events team to discuss your requirements and availability.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), debit cards, cash, and mobile payments including Apple Pay, Google Pay, and Samsung Pay. We also accept corporate credit cards and can provide detailed receipts for business expenses.

Do you offer catering services?

Yes! We provide full catering services for corporate events, private parties, and special occasions. Our catering menu includes our signature coffee beverages, pastries, lunch items, and dinner options. We can accommodate groups from 10 to 200+ guests. Contact us at catering@thevintagecafe.com for more information.

Can I purchase gift cards?

Absolutely! Gift cards are available in any denomination and can be purchased in-store or online. They make perfect gifts for coffee lovers and food enthusiasts. Our gift cards never expire and can be used for food, beverages, merchandise, and even private events.
