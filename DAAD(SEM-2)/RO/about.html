About AquaPure

Our Journey Towards Pure Water for All

Our Story

Founded in 2015, AquaPure emerged from a simple yet profound vision: to make clean, safe drinking water accessible to every household across the nation. What started as a small team of passionate engineers and water treatment specialists has grown into one of the leading manufacturers of RO water purification systems.

Our journey began when our founder, recognizing the critical need for reliable water purification solutions in both urban and rural areas, decided to combine cutting-edge technology with affordable pricing. We understood that clean water isn't a luxury—it's a fundamental human right that should be available to everyone, regardless of their economic background.

Over the years, we've continuously invested in research and development, working closely with water treatment experts, environmental scientists, and health professionals to create products that not only purify water but also retain essential minerals that are crucial for human health. Our commitment to innovation has led us to develop proprietary filtration technologies that set new industry standards.

Today, AquaPure serves over 500,000 satisfied customers across the country, from metropolitan cities to remote villages. Each installation represents not just a sale, but a step towards our ultimate goal: a world where every family has access to pure, healthy water straight from their tap.

Technology & Innovation

Understanding Our Advanced RO Purification Process

At AquaPure, we believe that understanding the technology behind water purification empowers our customers to make informed decisions about their health. Our advanced Reverse Osmosis (RO) systems combine multiple filtration stages to deliver the purest, safest drinking water possible.

The AquaPure 7-Stage Purification Process

1

Pre-Filtration

Removes large particles, sediments, and chlorine that could damage the delicate RO membrane. This stage extends the life of subsequent filters and improves overall system efficiency.

2

Carbon Block Filtration

Advanced carbon block technology removes chlorine, volatile organic compounds (VOCs), and improves taste and odor. This stage is crucial for protecting the RO membrane from chemical damage.

3

Reverse Osmosis Membrane

The heart of our system, featuring a semi-permeable membrane that removes 99.9% of dissolved impurities, including heavy metals, bacteria, viruses, and total dissolved solids (TDS).

4

Post-Carbon Filtration

Further polishes the water to ensure optimal taste and removes any residual odors. This stage guarantees that the water tastes fresh and clean with every sip.

5

Mineral Enhancement

Our proprietary mineral enhancement technology adds back essential minerals like calcium and magnesium that are beneficial for health, ensuring the water is not just pure but also healthy.

6

Alkaline Enhancement

Balances the pH of water to optimal alkaline levels (7.5-8.5 pH), which may help neutralize acid in the body and provide better hydration according to health research.

7

UV Sterilization

Final stage UV sterilization eliminates any remaining microorganisms and ensures 100% microbiologically safe water. This provides an additional layer of protection against waterborne diseases.
