How to Play

Controls:
  • Arrow Keys / WASD: Move Left & Right
  • Space / Up Arrow / W: Jump
  • P Key: Pause Game
  • R Key: Restart Game
Objective:
  • Collect golden coins to increase your score
  • Avoid or jump over enemies (Goombas & Koopas)
  • You have 3 lives - don't lose them all!
  • Survive as long as possible and beat the high score
  • Game gets harder as you progress
Tip: Master your jumps! Timing is everything when avoiding enemies and collecting coins!
