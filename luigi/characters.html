Meet the Characters

Discover the heroes and villains of the Mushroom Kingdom

Mario

Mario is the legendary hero of the Mushroom Kingdom and the most iconic plumber in gaming history. Known for his bravery, incredible jumping ability, and unwavering determination to save Princess Peach, Mario has been defeating Bowser and his minions for decades.

With his trademark red cap and overalls, Mario is always ready for adventure. His balanced stats make him the perfect choice for beginners and experts alike!

Luigi

Luigi is Mario's younger brother and a hero in his own right! Often overshadowed by his famous sibling, Luigi has proven time and time again that he's just as capable of saving the day. His signature green outfit and tall stature make him instantly recognizable.

Luigi is known for his exceptional jumping ability - even higher than Mario's! While he may be slightly timid, his courage shines through when his friends are in danger. He's the star of this game!

Princess Peach

Princess Peach is the beloved ruler of the Mushroom Kingdom, known for her kindness, grace, and royal elegance. Despite being frequently kidnapped by Bowser, she has proven herself to be strong and capable, often helping Mario and Luigi on their adventures.

With her iconic pink dress and golden crown, Princess Peach brings hope and light to the kingdom. She's not just a damsel in distress - she's a true leader with magical abilities and a caring heart!

Bowser

Bowser is the fearsome King of the Koopas and the primary antagonist of the Mushroom Kingdom. With his massive size, spiked shell, and fire-breathing abilities, he's a formidable foe for any hero. His obsession with kidnapping Princess Peach drives most of his evil schemes.

Despite being the villain, Bowser commands respect through his raw power and leadership of the Koopa Troop. His castle is filled with traps, lava, and loyal minions ready to stop Mario and Luigi!
