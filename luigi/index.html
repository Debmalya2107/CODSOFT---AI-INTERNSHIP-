About the Game

An Epic Platformer Adventure

Jump into the Mushroom Kingdom and experience a thrilling side-scrolling platformer game! Control Luigi as he runs, jumps, and dodges enemies while collecting golden coins.

Face challenging enemies like Goombas and Koopa Troopas, navigate through beautiful parallax backgrounds, and compete for the highest score on the leaderboard!

Game Features:

  • Smooth physics and controls
  • Multiple enemy types with AI
  • Beautiful parallax backgrounds
  • Coin collection system
  • Lives and scoring system
  • Global leaderboard
